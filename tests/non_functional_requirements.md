# Non-Functional Requirements

These non-functional requirements describe the quality and constraints of the Air Pollution & Health Impact Dashboard.

## NFR1 – Usability
The dashboard shall be easy to understand and use without specialist training.

## NFR2 – Performance
The dashboard shall respond to user filter selections within a reasonable time during normal use.

## NFR3 – Reliability
The dashboard shall handle empty or limited filtered results without crashing and shall display an appropriate message to the user.

## NFR4 – Consistency
The dashboard shall maintain a consistent visual style, including clear labels, readable text, and consistent spacing across sections.

## NFR5 – Accessibility of deployment
The dashboard shall be accessible through a web browser as a Streamlit application.
